Our Activites


Workshop: The Basics of C++

On 1 September 2018, a workshop on "The Basic of C++" was held in Room 226 from 10 AM onward. The workshop was open for all members. Interested members were present in the college uniform. They were able to learn a lot about the basics of C++. C++ iostream library, condition, loop, variable etc. were discussed in the workshop. The workshop was taken by Saran Debnath, Al Mubin Nabil and Shantanu Rahman, executives of NDITC.




Workshop: Microcontroller | The Basics

"Microcontroller | The Baics" was conducted by Khaled Bin Moinuddin, highly skilled trainer from Neophyte Nucleus on 06 October, 2018. 40 students forming 15 teams took part in the workshop. They got a satisfied knowledge about microcontroller. They were introduced with arduino uno, sonar sensor, breadboard, PIR sensor, buzzer, Bluetooth module, LED display and so on. They can now go on with different arduino projects.




Workshop: Graphic Designing

Graphic design is the process of visual communication and problem-solving through the use of typography, photography and illustration. Its popularity is spreading day by day. Thinking of its immense importance, a workshop was arranged on 01 January, 2019 where the members were given knowledge on the basics of Illusttrator. It was taken by Naimur Rahman Naim, Sectary of Publication of NDITC.




Seminar: Hour of Networking

From simple topics like how a computer connects to another computer, to the more sophisticated ones like what actually WiFi is and how it works, this all basic knowledge about the interesting world of Computer Networking was provided in a seminar which was held on March 06, 2019. It was conducted by Sakib Sadman Shajib and Md Zillur Rahman from Dept. of CSE, North South University.

Annual Fest: NDITC_INIT 2018


The very first IT festival ever in Notre Dame College was organized by NDITC on 23, 24 November 2018. 14 amazing and exciting events were there to meet the thirst for IT geeks. Students from more then 150 schools and colleges showed their outstanding performance in every events which they practised for year long. All the events and activities started following the inauguration ceremoney which witnessed the presence of Chief guest Dr. Fr. Leonard Shankar Rozario, CSC, Special guest Farhad Manjur and Club Moderator Saikat Lawrence Rozario.

The events that took place in this two days festival are listed here:
  • Hackathon
  • Line Follower Robot Competition
  • Robo Soccer
  • Programming Contest
  • IT Based Project Display
  • IT Based Wall Magazine Display
  • Digital Poster Designing
  • Web Page Designing
  • IT Based Presentation
  • Informatics Olympiad
  • IT Olympiad
  • Ideacon
  • Code Debugging
  • CTF

The ending ceremony started when all the events were succesfully held. The Chief guest Dr. Fr. Hemanto Pius Rozario, CSC (Principal of NDC), Special guest Mr. Jowel Rajoan Miah (Executive Director of Esquire Group) and Club Moderator Saikat Lawrence Rozario inspired the participants by their noteworthy speeches. After that the IT Geeks were given prizes for their outstanding performances in the events. This glorious festival came to an end following some marvellous cultural performances.
